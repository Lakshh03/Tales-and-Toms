The Waste Land

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

The Waste Land by T.S. Eliot is one of the most celebrated and enigmatic works of modernist poetry, published in 1922. This influential poem is known for its fragmented structure, extensive use of allusions, and exploration of themes such as disillusionment, decay, and the search for meaning in the post-World War I era.
