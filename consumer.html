At Tales and Tomes, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Consumer Health Data Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard any health-related data you may provide while using our services.

What is Consumer Health Data?

Consumer health data refers to any personal information that relates to an individual’s health status, medical history, or health-related behaviors. This may include data gathered through purchases of health-related books, participation in wellness programs, or surveys related to health topics.

How We Collect Consumer Health Data

We may collect consumer health data in the following ways:

  • Purchases: When you buy health-related books or products, we may collect information about your selections.
  • Surveys and Feedback: If you participate in surveys or provide feedback related to health topics, we may gather insights into your health interests and needs.
  • Account Creation: If you create an account, you may voluntarily provide health-related information.

How We Use Consumer Health Data

We may use your consumer health data for the following purposes:

  • Personalization: To tailor our recommendations and content based on your health interests.
  • Customer Support: To provide you with relevant assistance and support related to health-related products.
  • Research and Development: To improve our offerings and better understand our customers’ health-related interests.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your consumer health data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your consumer health data:

  • Access: You can request access to the health data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your consumer health data, subject to certain legal obligations.

Sharing of Consumer Health Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your consumer health data to third parties. We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Consumer Health Data Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for updates.
