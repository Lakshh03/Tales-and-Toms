Fourth Wing

Rebecca Yarros

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros is a fantasy novel that immerses readers into a world where young dragon riders train and compete for their place in society. The story follows the protagonist, Violet Sorrengail, as she navigates the challenges of the war college, bonds with a dragon, and faces dangerous trials. Packed with action, romance, and intricate world-building, the book captures the trials and triumphs of becoming a dragon rider in a richly imagined universe.
