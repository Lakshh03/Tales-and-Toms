Beloved

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

"Beloved" by Toni Morrison is a powerful and haunting novel set after the American Civil War. The story revolves around Sethe, a former slave who lives in a house haunted by the ghost of her dead daughter. The novel explores themes of memory, trauma, and the enduring impact of slavery on individuals and families
