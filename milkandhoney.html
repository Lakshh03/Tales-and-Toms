Milk and Honey

By Rupi Kaur

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

Milk and Honey by Rupi Kaur is a deeply personal collection of poetry and prose that delves into themes of love, loss, trauma, healing, and femininity. Published in 2014, the book is divided into four sections: The Hurting, The Loving, The Breaking, and The Healing. Each section serves a different purpose and explores different emotions, reflecting the journey of pain and healing.
