Ariel

By Sylvia Plath

★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

Ariel is a posthumous collection of poems by Sylvia Plath, first published in 1965, two years after her death. This collection is considered one of the most significant works in 20th-century poetry. The poems in Ariel are known for their intensity, vivid imagery, and emotional depth, dealing with themes such as identity, trauma, mental illness, and the struggles of the female experience.
