Life of Pi

By Yann Martel

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)

"Life of Pi" by Yann Martel is a captivating novel that follows the incredible journey of Piscine Molitor "Pi" Patel, a young Indian boy who survives a shipwreck and is stranded on a lifeboat in the Pacific Ocean. Pi's companions on this harrowing journey include a Bengal tiger named Richard Parker, a zebra, a hyena, and an orangutan. The story delves into themes of survival, faith, and the human spirit's resilience in the face of overwhelming odds.
